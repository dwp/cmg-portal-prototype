Step 6 - Shared Care

We work out the average number of nights per week your child stays overnight with the other parent. If your child stays overnight with them for at least 52 nights a year, the child maintenance they pay is reduced. This is an average of 1 night a week.

How we work out shared care reductions depends on the number of nights your child stays overnight with the other parent.
