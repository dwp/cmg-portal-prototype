Step 6 - Shared Care

We work out the average number of nights per week your child stays overnight with you. If your child stays overnight with you for at least 52 nights a year, the child maintenance you pay is reduced. This is an average of 1 night a week.

How we work out shared care reductions depends on the number of nights your child stays overnight with you.
