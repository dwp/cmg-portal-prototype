Missed payments

How we deal with missed payments depends on which service you use.

Collect and Pay service

The Child Maintenance Service will recognise when a payment is missed and appropriate action will be considered to recover the payment.

Direct pay service

You should try and resolve a missed payment by contacting the other parent. If this is not possible, the receiving parent needs to ask the Child Maintenance Service to take action.

This could result in you having to use our collect and pay service and you will need to pay the collection fees.
