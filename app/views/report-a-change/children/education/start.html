Education

Reporting a new child may affect the amount of child maintenance you pay or receive. We need to know if:

  • you have a new child, who qualifies for child maintenance, with a parent you already have a child maintenance case with
  • the paying parent is financially supporting a new child, who is not part of an existing child maintenance case

You will need to provide evidence of any children you are supporting. Evidence can include a copy of child benefit payments, a legal document or a letter proving they are in full time education.
