What happens next

Once we have reviewed your request and received your evidence, we will contact the other parent to confirm the details you’ve given us.

If there is:

  • a change to your child maintenance payments, we will send you a new expected payment plan advising how much child maintenance you will receive and by when
  • no change to your child maintenance payments we will let you know

You need to upload your evidence

Evidence can include, for example a copy of child benefit payments, a legal document or a letter proving they are in full time education.

If you don’t have your evidence available now, you have 14 days to upload your documents, go to ‘Messages’ and select ‘Upload documents’.

Check the progress of the change you have reported (it may take up to 30 seconds for your change to appear).

Remember to check we have your correct telephone number in case we need to contact you, go to 'Details' and select 'Contact details'.
