How to sign in to your parent or guardian case

If you already have a case with the Child Maintenance Service, you can manage it online.

To sign into your Child Maintenance account, you will need your:

  • 12 digit customer reference number (this begins with '121'. Find this on letters we sent you about your case)
  • National Insurance number (it’s on your National Insurance card, benefit letter, payslip or P60. For example, ‘QQ 12 34 56 C’)
  • 7 digit pin (this was set up when you started using the Child Maintenance Service. You can ask for a reminder if you’re not sure)

If you have forgotten or do not know your PIN, you can reset it.

Once signed in, you can report a change of circumstance, view your child maintenance arrangement, update personal information, contact us and much more.
