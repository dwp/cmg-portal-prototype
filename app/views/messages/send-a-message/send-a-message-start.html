Send a message

You can send a message to the Child Maintenance Service if you:

  • are responding to a request from us
  • need to tell us something that cannot be reported online
  • have a specific query about your case

It can take up to 7 days to review your message.

Before you start

For changes to your circumstances, it is usually faster to report a change online. This includes reporting a missed payment.

If you have a general query, you can find more information about managing your case online (opens in a new tab). and how we calculate your payments (opens in a new tab).
